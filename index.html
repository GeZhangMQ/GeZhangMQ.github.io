BIOGRAPHY

Ge Zhang is a lecturer at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Donghua University. She earned her Ph.D. from the School of Computing, Macquarie University in 2024. Her research focuses on developing machine learning algorithms for graph-structured data, with a particular interest in applications such as anomaly detection.

During her Ph.D. studies, she was honored as one of the 2022 Microsoft Research PhD Fellows, being the sole recipient of this prestigious award from Australia and New Zealand in 2022.
