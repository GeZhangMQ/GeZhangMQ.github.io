BIOGRAPHY

Ge Zhang is a lecturer at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Donghua University. She completed her Ph.D. at the School of Computing, Macquarie University in 2024. During her Ph.D. studies, she was honored as one of the 2022 Microsoft Research PhD Fellows, being the sole recipient of this prestigious award from Australia and New Zealand in 2022. Her research focuses on AI-safety, Graph Nueral Networks and Anomaly Detection.

张鸽，博士，东华大学计算机科学与技术学院讲师，入选上海市浦江人才(A类)。 2024年于澳大利亚麦考瑞大学获得博士学位，在攻读博士期间，荣获Microsoft Research Phd Fellowship Award (微软学者奖)。研究主要集中在人工智能安全、图机器学习算法、异常检测等领域。目前已在中国计算机协会(CCF)推荐A类以及B类期刊/会议上发表多篇论文。欢迎对以上研究感兴趣的准研究生或本科生加入团队。
