BIOGRAPHY

Ge Zhang, Ph.D., is a lecturer at the College of Information and Intelligent Science, Donghua University, and a recipient of the Shanghai Pujiang Talent Program. She earned her Ph.D. in Computer Science from Macquarie University, Australia, in 2024, and was awarded the Microsoft Research PhD Fellowship Award in 2022 (the only recipient in the Australia & New Zealand region that year). Her research interests include Anomaly Detection and Large Language Models. Prospective graduate or undergraduate students with an interest in the above research topics are welcome to join her team.

张鸽，博士，东华大学信息与智能科学学院讲师，上海市浦江人才。 2024年于澳大利亚麦考瑞大学获得计算机科学博士学位，2022年获Microsoft Research Phd Fellowship Award (微软学者奖，2022年澳大利亚&新西兰地区唯一获奖者)。研究兴趣为异常检测、大语言模型等领域。目前已在相关领域中国计算机协会(CCF)推荐A类会议及SCI 1区期刊上发表多篇论文。欢迎对以上研究感兴趣的准研究生或本科生加入团队。
